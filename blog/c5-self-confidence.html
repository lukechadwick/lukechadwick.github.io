Cultural Blog Part 5

Self Confidence

2 July 2018

What is the relationship between mindfulness and self awareness?
I feel they are more or less the same, or at least very similar, to be mindful requires self-awareness and vice-versa.
Try at least one of the self awareness practices from the chapter (Body Scan, Journaling). What was your experience of it? How did you feel?
I did the body scan, I felt it was nice and relaxing, but it's quite cold here at the moment so that was quite distracting. Perhaps in a warmer environment I could enjoy the full effect.
What did you take away from the "My Emotions are Not Me" section?
I think anyone of reasonable intelligence grasps this concept naturally. Although we all 'feel' emotions and perhaps sometimes let them get the better of us, how we act on and regulate them is more important.
