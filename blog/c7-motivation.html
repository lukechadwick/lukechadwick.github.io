How does life look through the 'pleasure, passion and purpose' lens?
Pleasure is almost high like state that is unsustainable long term, passion or 'flow',is more desirable as we are less likely to habituate from the happimness that we get from it and is thus more sustainable, higher purpose even more so as it can become the source of sustainable happiness that we desire.
From the 'discovering values and higher purpose' exercise, What are your core values? What do you stand for? ?
Honesty, hard work, helping others, free speech (even if I disagree), having fun and not taking things too seriously.

How did you find the resilience ideas and meditation exercise?
I liked the part about congnitive resilience, specifically how we explain our setbacks to ourselves and creating mental habits to develop optimism.
